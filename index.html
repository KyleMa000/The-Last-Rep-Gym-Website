The Last Rep Fitness

Introduction

Andrei Selenskikh, the owner of Last Rep Fitness Co, a proud father of two sons, originally born in Russia, built his strong muscles and tough mind in the Russian navy, and has been helping others building better shape and living a healthier life for 16 years after settling in NYC. The spirit of The Last Rep is to treat every rep like the last. Constantly pushing oneself out of the comfort zone relentlessly. It is to believe hard work beats talent and only hard work could bring satisfying results for one. Join the Gym today and bring a change to your life.
